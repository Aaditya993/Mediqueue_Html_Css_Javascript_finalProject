Token Booking

How do I book an OPD token?
Booking a token is simple. Navigate to Live Queue or Departments, choose your department, click "Book Token", enter your name, select Standard or Fast-Track, and confirm. Your unique token number is generated instantly and shown on screen — no registration required.
What is the difference between Standard and Fast-Track tokens?
Standard tokens are free and enter the normal queue. Fast-Track tokens (⚡) are a paid upgrade that places you ahead of standard queue patients — ideal when you have limited time or need priority consultation. Fast-Track slots are limited per session. The additional fee varies by department and is shown before you confirm.
Can I book a token for someone else?
Yes. When booking, enter the patient's name in the name field. The token will be issued in that name. Please ensure the patient arrives at the hospital before their token is called — tokens cannot be paused or held. You can track the queue position using the Live Queue page.
How do I cancel my token?
On the Live Queue page, find your active token in the "My Token" panel on the right sidebar and click "Cancel Token". Cancellation is immediate. If you paid for a Fast-Track token, please refer to the Refund Policy section below. Standard tokens are free and carry no cancellation penalty.
What happens if I miss my turn?
If your token number is called and you are not present, the system automatically skips to the next patient. You have a 5-minute grace window before your token expires. If you miss your slot, please visit the OPD reception desk directly — staff can re-queue you manually, subject to doctor availability.

Queue & Wait Times

How accurate are the wait time estimates?
Wait times are estimates based on current queue length, average consultation duration for that department, and real-time throughput. They are updated every 30 seconds. Actual wait may vary due to emergencies, complex cases, or doctor breaks. We recommend arriving 5–10 minutes before your estimated slot.
How often does the queue refresh?
The Live Queue dashboard auto-refreshes every 30 seconds. A countdown timer and progress bar on the page shows when the next refresh will occur. You can also observe the "Updated at" timestamp at the top of the queue list.
Why does a department show "Closed"?
Departments may be closed due to planned maintenance, doctor unavailability, public holidays, or emergency situations. Check the Alerts page for closure announcements with expected reopening times. Closed departments will not accept new tokens; you can check back the following working day or contact reception.

Payments & Fees

Where can I see the consultation fees?
Full consultation fees for all 12 departments are listed on the Departments & Pricing page. Each department card shows the Standard fee and Fast-Track fee side by side. There are no hidden charges — the amount shown is what you pay at the counter.
Is there a refund if I cancel a Fast-Track token?
Refunds for Fast-Track cancellations are processed as follows: Full refund if cancelled more than 30 minutes before your estimated slot. 50% refund if cancelled within 30 minutes of your slot. No refund if you were a no-show after your token was called. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method. Contact the billing counter or support team with your token ID.
What payment methods are accepted?
Consultation fees are paid directly at the hospital billing counter. Accepted methods include Cash, Debit/Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Net Banking. Insurance and cashless claims — please bring your insurance card and policy number to the counter for processing.

Patient Account

Do I need to create an account to book a token?
No account is required for basic token booking. You only need to enter your name. However, creating a patient account unlocks additional features: visit history, saved preferences, SMS/email notifications when your token is nearly due, and faster future bookings with pre-filled details.
Can I see my past visit history?
Visit history (dates, departments, doctors consulted) is maintained for registered patients in their account dashboard. If you booked as a guest, your history is tied to the browser session only. For a complete medical history and records, please contact the Medical Records department at the hospital directly.

Technical Issues

The queue isn't loading. What should I do?
First, check your internet connection. Then try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac). If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or opening the page in a private/incognito window. If all departments show as unavailable, there may be a scheduled maintenance window — check the Alerts page for notifications.
My token was booked but I can't see it on the queue page.
Token data is stored in your browser session. If you close the tab, switch browsers, or clear browser data, the "My Token" panel may not display your booking. Your token is still valid at the hospital — note down your Token ID (e.g. MQ-GEN-260301-041) when booking and present it at the OPD reception desk if needed.
Is MediQueue available on mobile?
Yes — MediQueue is fully responsive and works on all modern smartphones and tablets via your mobile browser. A dedicated mobile app for iOS and Android is currently in development and expected to launch soon. In the meantime, you can add the website to your home screen for an app-like experience.
